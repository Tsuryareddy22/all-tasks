0.1Budget & Bidding

We review pricing by seeking bids from additional vendors as needed. A summary of pricing and lead times is provided for your approval, ensuring you have all the information needed. Before any orders are placed, we submit a final budget for approval, ensuring clarity and alignment on costs before proceeding.

0.2Ordering & Logistics

We manage the full ordering process, from issuing purchase orders and collecting invoices to submitting them for payment. Our team tracks samples and prototypes for approval, provides order updates, and participates in weekly OAC meetings. We handle product inspections, resolving any issues or managing returns as needed.

0.3Delivery & Install

We coordinate deliveries with the warehouse, general contractor, and ownership, ensuring a smooth process. Our team oversees product installation, providing site observations and addressing any vendor issues. After installation, we collaborate with design and construction teams to prepare a final punch list, ensuring all details are handled.

0.4Project Accounting

Invoices are submitted as scheduled, either for you to pay vendors or for Avenir to manage payments. We track budget vs. actual costs and provide reports. Throughout the project, we monitor product costs, taxes, and deliveries. At project close, we ensure all payments are made and provide documentation confirming no outstanding balances.
